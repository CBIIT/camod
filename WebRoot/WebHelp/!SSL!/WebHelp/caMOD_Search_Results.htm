Advanced Search Mode

 

  • Once you click the Search button, search results display in the Search Results viewer.

 

  • If you click the Search button having defined no parameters for the search, all of the models in the database will be listed in the Search Results.

 

  • Click any of the models listed in the Search Results viewer to open the model and its submitted information.

 

Once you click on the model, the model information menu options at the upper left of the page display. Only the options that contain model characteristics/data for your selected model are underlined.

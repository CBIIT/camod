Administration Roles

 

Note: The Administration Roles page is accessible only if you have Coordinator/Editor/Screener privileges.

 

The Administration Roles viewer opens when you click on the Admin tab in the caMOD viewer. This viewer lists models that need screening, and it also lists screening and editing assignments.  All available links for the Admin section display in the upper left-hand corner of the viewer.

 

  • If you are the Administrator, click on the associated Assign button(s) to open the dialog box where you can make the assignments.

 

  • In the Models to Screen section, the list displays model assignments. Click the model name which is a link to open the Model Characteristics page for the model.

 

  • Review the page. Click the Place your Comment(s) Here hypertext link to open a text box where you can enter your comments.

 

  • Click on any underlined category links in the Model Details section in the upper left-hand corner of the viewer. (The underline format indicates that category contains data about the model.) Review the material that opens.

 

  • Return to the Administration Roles page. In the Models to Screen section, click the Approve or Reject link, indicating your final evaluation of the model.

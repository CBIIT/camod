Coordinating the Review Process

 

The Coordinator manages the model review process. The responsibilities in this process include:

 

Assign Functions/Grant Privileges for Screeners and Editors

 

To assign functions or to grant privileges, the Coordinator must select User Appointment from the menu on the left-hand side.

 

Appointing Screeners and Editors

  • To appoint Screeners and Editors, the Coordinator checks the box in front of the name and clicks the appropriate button. Once the person becomes a Screener or Editor, the table column Member Status contains the matching entry .

  • To see all Screeners or Editors, select the function from the drop-down list in the right-hand corner of the page and click Go.

 

Review Process

 

To control the review process, the Coordinator selects Model Assignment from the menu on the left-hand side.

 

  1. After a model has been entered, the Coordinator assigns a Screener who checks that all required fields have been specified and that the model does not contain junk or grossly inappropriate material.

  2. The Coordinator is  informed about the decision of the Screener by email.

  3. If the Screener accepts the model, the Coordinator then assigns the model to an Editor, who is a content expert.

 

 

Assigning Models to Screeners

Only completed models can be screened.

  1. To see models that need to be assigned for screening, the Coordinator selects Completed - Not Screened models from the drop-down list.

  2. The Coordinator marks the models by clicking the check box in front of the model name.

  3. The Coordinator selects a Screener from the drop-down list for the selected models.

 

Assigning Models to Editors

Only models that were approved by the Screener can be send to the Editor.

  1. To see models that need to be reviewed, the Coordinator selects Screened - Approved models from the drop-down list.

  2. The Coordinator marks the models by clicking the check box in front of the model name.

  3. The Coordinator selects an Editor from the drop down list for the selected models.

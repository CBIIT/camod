caMOD Permissions

 

The following categories and their associated authorizations are found in caMOD:

 

  • User--Includes every user without an account. These users can query the database without restrictions, but they cannot submit models or comment on models.

 

  • Registered User:Can submit models to the database and can comment on models submitted by other researchers.

 

  • Super User--Has access to all model entries to update them. This is to facilitate minor text corrections in an entry without having to bother the submitter.

 

  • Screener--Registered user who performs the functions of a Screener.

 

  • Editor--Registered user who performs the functions of an Editor.

 

  • Coordinator--Registered user who performs the functions of a Coordinator.

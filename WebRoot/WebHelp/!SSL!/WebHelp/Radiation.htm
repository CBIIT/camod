Radiation

To enter or edit radiation for an animal model:

 

  1. Click Carcinogenic Interventions from the list of Submit and Edit Models menu options on the left side of the caMOD viewing window. These menu options only display after you have clicked the (model) Submit button in other caMOD viewers.

 

  1. Click the Enter Radiation red hypertext link. This opens the Radiation form.

 

  1. Enter or select the appropriate information about your animal model in the text entry fields. Items with an asterisk are required fields.

 

Radiation Fields

Description

*Radiation

Select the type of radiation from the drop-down list. If the appropriate Radiation is not listed, select Other from the drop-down list and enter the Other Radiation in the corresponding text field.

Other Radiation

Enter Radiation that is not listed in the drop-down list.

Dose

Enter the dose of the Radiation given. Select the dosage units from the drop-down menu.

Administrative Routes

Select the course in which a drug was administered in order to reach the site of action in the body.  If the appropriate route is not listed, select Other from the drop-down list and enter the Other Administrative Route in the corresponding text field.

Other Administrative Routes

Enter Administrative Route that is not listed in the drop-down list.

Treatment Regimen

Enter times and intervals in which the treatment was administered.

Age

Enter age information in the text box and from the drop-down list.

Gender

Select gender from the drop-down list.

 

  1. Click the Submit button to execute the submission of the model information. Click Reset to clear the form.

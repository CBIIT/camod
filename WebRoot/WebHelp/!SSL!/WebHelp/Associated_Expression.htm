Associated Expression

Once you have submitted data to the Engineered Transgene, Genomic Segment or the Targeted Modification pages, the entry is listed under the Engineered Transgene, Genomic Segment or the Targeted Modification categories in the Submit and Edit Models listing on the left-hand side of the browser. The Enter Associated Expression option displays (in brown text, indicating an active option) under the entered item.

 

To enter or edit associated expression for a model:

 

  1. Click Enter Assoc[iated] Expression.

 

  1. Enter or select the appropriate information about your animal model in the text entry fields. Items with an asterisk are required fields.

 

Associated Expression Fields

Description

Organ/Tissue

Click the Select button. Select from the Organ/Tissue vocabulary tree that opens the organ or tissue in which the transgene, the genomic segment or the targeted modification was found.

Expression Level

Select from the list of expression descriptions in the drop-down menu how the transgene, the genomic segment or the targeted modification affected the expression level.

 

  1. Click the Submit button to execute the submission of the model information. Click Reset to clear the form.

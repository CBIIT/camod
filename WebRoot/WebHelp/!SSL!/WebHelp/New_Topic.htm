Controlled Vocabulary for Organs and Tissues

The tissue selection tree is used on multiple pages in the search and submission process.
Clicking the folder icon opens or closes the folders.
To select an item click the on the linked name.
Confirm your selection or if you accidentially selected a wrong term, hit "Cancel"
To find organs or tissues or synoyms of those enter the term in the search field on the left site of the page and hit the "Search" button. The search results will appear in the lower left frame.
The item can be selected by clicking the hyperlinked name displayed in the lower left frame or it can be highlighted in the tree-like structure in the right frame and can then be selected from there.

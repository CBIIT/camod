Public Member

 

Any interested person can become a public member of caMOD.

 

To become a member, you must enter personal information such as name and email address before you can submit a record to the database. Once you submit a record, you automatically a "public member" of caMOD.

 

  • Submitting to the database:

Public members have the right to submit records, and to edit their own records.

 

  • Querying the database:

When a public member queries the database the database will return records that the submitter defined as "public".

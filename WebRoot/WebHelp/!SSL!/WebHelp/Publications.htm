Publications

To enter or edit  the information about publications:

 

  1. Click Publications from the list of Submit and Edit Models menu options on the left side of the caMOD viewing window. These menu options only display after you have clicked the (model) Submit button in other caMOD viewers.

 

  1. Click the Enter Publication red hypertext link. This opens the Publications form.

 

  1. Enter or select the appropriate information about your animal model in the text entry fields. Items with an asterisk are required fields.

 

Publications Fields

Description

*First Author

Primary author for the publication

Publication Status

 Select Published or Unpublished. Unpublished can include work that is submitted or in preparation.

Publication where reported first time?

Is this the publication where the animal model being entered was described for the first time? Select Yes or No.

PubMed ID (PMID)

Enter the PubMed ID (PMID) or click the link to look up the PubMed ID. Enter it in the appropriate field. Click the Fill In Fields button. caMOD automatically retrieves the publication information based on the PubMed ID and populates the Publications form fields identified in this table with bullets.

 

Note: For books and journals not listed in PubMed, enter the appropriate information on this form.

  • Title of Publication

  • Year of Publication

  • Journal

  • Volume

  • Start Page

  • End Page

These fields are populated automatically when a publication is identified with the PubMed ID and the Fill In Fields button is clicked. See previous row in this table.

 

 If you cannot or do not locate the publication on PubMed, enter this information manually.

 

  1. Click the Submit button to send the publication to the database.

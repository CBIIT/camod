Associated Expression/Targeted Modification

This page opens after you submit data to the Targeted Modification page.

 

This page allows you to specify the Organ(s) in which the Targeted Modification was found and how it affected the level of gene or gene product expression. define expression levels of the Targeted Modification in specific organs or tissues.

 

Click Select to open the navigation tree in which you can locate the Organ/Tissue/Cell Type in which the gene and expression modification occurred.

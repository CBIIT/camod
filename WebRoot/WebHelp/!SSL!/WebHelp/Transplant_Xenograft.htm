Transplant/Xenograft

To enter transplant/xenograft information to a submitted model,

 

  1. Click Xenograft from the list of Submit and Edit Models menu options on the left side of the caMOD viewing window. These menu options only display after you have clicked the (model) Submit button in other caMOD viewers.

 

  1. Click the Enter Transplant/Xenograft heading.

 

  1. In the Xenograft/Transplantation dialog box, enter or select the appropriate information about your animal model in the text entry fields. Items with an asterisk are required fields.

 

Xenograft/Transplantation  Field

Description

*Cell Line/Transplant

Enter the name of the Transplant or Cell Line. In some cases, this is the name of the cell line that is being inserted.

ATTC Number (if available)

Enter the unique identifier of the cell line in the American Type Culture Collection (ATCC). If you don't know the number, click the Find ATCC # button. Once you have found the number, copy the number and paste it onto the field on the Transplant/Xenograft page.

Species/Strain

Select the Species or Strain of the Transplant from the drop-down list. If the appropriate Chemical/Drug is not listed, select Other from the drop-down list and enter the Other Chemical/Drug in the text field under the list.

Parental Cell Line

Enter the name or designator of the parental cell line to be used in the experiment.

Amount of Cells

Enter the number or amount of cells transplanted into the animal.

Mice Age

Enter the age of the animal.

Tumor Harvest Date after Transplant

Enter the number of days after transplantation when the tumor was harvested from the animal.

 

Method of Modification

Describe the modification method(s) used for the cell line or transplant.

Genetic Alteration

Describe the genetic manipulations used in the transplant or xenograft.

*Host Species

 Select the Host Species from the drop-down list.

Host Strain

A specific breeding population of mice, including inbred strains, progeny of specific genetic crosses, or a mixed population of mice with partially known or unknown genetic background. (Source: Jackson Laboratory MGI Glossary)

 

If applicable, select from the drop-down list. If the appropriate Host Strain is not listed, select Other and enter the Other Host Strain in the text field under the list.

If Other Strain

Enter Host Strain that is not listed in the drop-down list.

Site of Administration

Select from the drop-down list the the body part in which the graft was placed (intraperitoneal, subcutaneous).

*Graft Type

Select from the drop-down list. If the appropriate Graft Type is not listed, select Other from the drop-down list and enter the Other Graft Type in the following text box.

If Other Graft Type

Enter Graft Type  that is not listed in the drop-down list.

 

  1. Click the Submit button to add the information to your model. Click Reset to clear the dialog box.

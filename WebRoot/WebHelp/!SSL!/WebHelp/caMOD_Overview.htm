caMOD Overview

 

 

For each new model it is necessary to complete the information on the "Model Characteristics page". Required fields on this page are:

  • Model Descriptor
  • Species of the Model
  • Phenotype
  • Record Release Date
 
 
You must fill out only the parts that apply to your model, e.g. if your model was not exposed to carcinogenic interventions you don't have to enter data about carcinogenic interventions.

You can make multiple entries for each category, e.g. if the model was exposed to two different chemicals or if the model was used in multiple therapeutical trials.

If you cannot complete the submission of the model in one session, you can come back later and add data. We will provide you with a list of all models that you entered after you have logged in.

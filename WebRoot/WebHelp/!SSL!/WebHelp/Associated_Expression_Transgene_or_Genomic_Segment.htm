Associated Expression/Transgene or Genomic Segment

This page opens after you submit data to the Engineered Transgene page or the Genomic Segment page.   

 

This page allows you to specify the Organ in which the Transgene or Genomic Segment was expressed; you also can indicate the level of  expression detected.

 

Click Select to open the navigation tree in which you can locate the Organ/Tissue/Cell Type in which the Transgene or Genomic Segment was inserted and expressed.

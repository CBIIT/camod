Controlled Vocabularies for Organs and Diagnoses

The selected organ is transferred on the search page or histopathology submission page.
The diagnosis vocabulary opens in a new browser window after the user clicks the "Select" button next to the diagnosis field.
On opening the diagnosis tree displays the diagnoses related to the previously selected organ. In this example mammary gland diagnoses are shown. To navigate to a specific diagnosis open the folder(s).
A diagnosis can be selected by clicking the hyperlinked name.
After confirming the selected diagnosis it is inserted into the field on the search or the histopathology page.
Diagnoses can be search for bye entering a term in the search field in the upper left frame and hitting the search button. The search results are displayed in the lower left frame and can be selected by clicking on the diagnosis or highlighted in the tree.
What to do when you do not find the term you are looking for
If you don't find the term you are looking for by either searching the tree or using the search, click on the "Show all Diagnoses" link to display the complete tree.
Once the whole tree is displayed, enter your search term again and search the vocabulary. It is time consuming and therefore not advisable to manually search the tree by opening folders.
If the search does not retrieve satisfying results you may submit a new diagnostic term. This term/phrase will be stored with your record and it will also be forwarded to the Vocabulary Team to be considered for permanent inclusion in the vocabulary.
To enter a new term click the "Submit button" as indicated by the red arrow.
A pop-up window will open and you can enter the new term/phrase.

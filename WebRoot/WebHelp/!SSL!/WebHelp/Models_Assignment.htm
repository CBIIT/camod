Models Assignment

 

Note: The Models Assignment page is accessible only if you have Coordinator/Editor/Screener privileges.

 

The Administration Roles page displays two lists:

 

Models that have been assigned to the logged in user for screening, and models waiting for screening assignments.

 

  1. If you are the Coordinator, click the Assign button that corresponds to models needing Screener assignments.

 

  1. Select from the drop-down menu the Screener to whom the model is to be assigned.

 

  1. In the Notes text field, enter any appropriate comments you have for the Screener.

 

Click the Submit button to execute the assignment of the model information. Click Reset to clear the form.
